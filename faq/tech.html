What is “Tech”?

Tech is one of the 4 core stats in Fortnite: Save the World, it mainly affects how strong your traps and tech‑based systems are. In this case, you want higher Tech so that your traps do more damage, which is crucial for this base since it relies completely on damage traps.

To increase your Tech, you’ll need to focus on leveling up your Commander Level, aswell as your 4th and 8th survivor squads.
